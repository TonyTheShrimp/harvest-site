Harvest — Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 24, 2026

Harvest is a browser extension that inspects a website's front-end resources (HTML, CSS, JavaScript, images, fonts, and other assets), can explore multiple pages of a site, and lets you export a local, offline copy. This page explains exactly what Harvest does and does not do with data.

The short version: Harvest does not collect, store, sell, or transmit any personal data. Everything it does happens locally, inside your own browser and on your own device. There are no analytics, no tracking, and no external servers operated by Harvest.

What data Harvest accesses

  • Web page content of sites you actively act on. When you click Scan Page or Explore Site, Harvest reads the current page (and, for crawls, other same-origin pages of that site) to discover and collect their resources. This only happens in response to your explicit action.
  • Resource files of that site. To build a faithful offline copy, Harvest fetches the site's own assets (stylesheets, scripts, images, fonts, etc.).

How that data is used

  • Collected page content and assets are processed entirely on your device, to display them in the extension's side panel and to build the ZIP archive you choose to download.
  • Nothing is sent to Harvest's developer or to any third party. Harvest has no backend server.
  • Harvest does not use cookies, advertising IDs, analytics SDKs, or any tracking technology.

What Harvest stores

  • Harvest does not maintain a remote database and does not store your browsing history. The only files saved are the ZIP archives you explicitly export, which are written to your computer's normal Downloads location by your browser.

Permissions and why they are needed

  • sidePanel — to show Harvest's interface as a docked side panel.
  • scripting & tabs — to run the resource-discovery logic on the page you scan and to open/manage temporary background tabs while crawling additional pages of the same site.
  • Host access to websites (<all_urls>) — because Harvest is a general-purpose site inspector, it must be able to read and fetch resources from whatever site you choose to inspect. This access is used only to perform the scan/export you request, and only on sites you act on.

Third-party services

None. Harvest does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or data services. The optional local web server that Harvest bundles inside an exported ZIP runs only on your own machine, when you choose to launch it, to let the saved site render offline.

Children's privacy

Harvest is a developer/designer utility and is not directed at children. It collects no personal information from anyone.
